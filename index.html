Organic Matter
pH 6.2 is suitable for most native species. Supplement with compost at 25 kg/m² in planting beds to raise OC above 1.5%.
Percolation Enhancement
Percolation trenches (600mm wide, 1.5m deep, gravel-filled) at 15m intervals across the catchment.
Biochar Amendment
